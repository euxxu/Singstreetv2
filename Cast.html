Casting

In a July 2014 interview, Carney announced he would be casting unknown actors in the film.[12][13] The unknown actors turned out to be Ferdia Walsh-Peelo, Ben Carolan, Mark McKenna, Percy Chamburuka, Conor Hamilton, Karl Rice, and Ian Kenny.[14] In September 2014, it was announced that Aidan Gillen, Maria Doyle Kennedy, and Jack Reynor had joined the cast of the film, portraying the role of Conor's father, mother and brother respectively.

  • Ferdia Walsh-Peelo as Conor "Cosmo" Lawlo
  • Lucy Boynton as Raphina
  • Jack Reynor as Brendan Lawlor
  • Aidan Gillen as Robert Lawlor
  • Maria Doyle Kennedy as Penny Lawlor
  • Kelly Thornton as Ann Lawlor
  • Ben Carolan as Darren Mulvey
  • Mark McKenna as Eamon
  • Percy Chamburuka as Ngig
  • Conor Hamilton as Larry
  • Karl Rice as Garry
  • Ian Kenny as Barry Bray
